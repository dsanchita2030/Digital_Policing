INSTRUCTION:The owner of any motor vechile stolen in the state must make a report in writing to the office of the local or state police in any municipality in which the theft occurred(C.G.S 14-151a(a)).The filing of this report in accordance with the above,is required by C.G.S 14-151a(b) prior to settlement of the insurence claim.

              NOTICE:A person who knowingly makes a false report of the theft of a motor vechile to a police officer shall be fined five hundred dollers or imprisoned for not more than six months or both in accordance with section 14-198 of the connecticut general statutes.
